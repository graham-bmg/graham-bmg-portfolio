001 — The Artist

The
Story
So Far

Graham BMG is the musical identity of Ogheneochuko Prosper Emeyoma-Atigbi — a singer, songwriter, and producer born in Warri, Nigeria, now based in Lagos, crafting what he calls cinematic soul: AfroR&B built on West African rhythm, emotional depth, and the kind of intimacy that doesn't need to be loud to be felt.

His music moves like a mood — warm, layered, unhurried — and draws you in the way a great film score does, except the subject is always the heart. He produces everything himself, keeps his public persona deliberately private, and releases independently on all major platforms.

His latest single Distance — sung across English, Yoruba, Pidgin, and Spanish — extends that intimacy further, reflecting both his Nigerian roots and a reaching outward toward something universal.

“He doesn't just make music. He constructs worlds.”

— Artist Statement
